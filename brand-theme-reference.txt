// Brand-Matched Theme Example - Customize colors based on your logo

// If your logo is primarily GREEN:
bg-gradient-to-b from-emerald-900 via-slate-900 to-emerald-900

// If your logo is primarily RED:
bg-gradient-to-b from-red-900 via-slate-900 to-red-900

// If your logo is primarily PURPLE:
bg-gradient-to-b from-purple-900 via-slate-900 to-purple-900

// If your logo is primarily TEAL:
bg-gradient-to-b from-teal-900 via-slate-900 to-teal-900

// Multi-color logo (use dominant color):
bg-gradient-to-b from-[#YOUR-LOGO-COLOR] via-slate-900 to-[#YOUR-LOGO-COLOR]

// Steps to implement:
1. Extract the dominant color from your logo
2. Replace the blue-* classes with your chosen color
3. Update hover states and active states to match
4. Ensure sufficient contrast for accessibility
